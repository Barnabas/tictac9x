TicTac 9X, a.k.a. "Ultimate Tic Tac Toe" is tic tac toe meets Inception. Read this for how to play and more information.

After you start a game, you can send a link to a friend to join you. You'll be X, they'll be O. They have the first move because that's polite.

If you're just looking to test this out, you'll have to use two separate web browsers opened to the same URL.
